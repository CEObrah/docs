The Student Portal is your personal space for managing your learning journey. It is designed to help you stay organized, understand your progress, and get the support you need to succeed.

## Your Personal AI Agent

When you log in to the Student Portal, you will be greeted by your personal AI agent. This is your dedicated tutor, coach, and study partner. It is a powerful tool that is designed to help you achieve your academic goals.
