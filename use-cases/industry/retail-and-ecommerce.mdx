The MindLab platform provides a suite of tools for the retail and eCommerce industries. Our platform is designed to help you to improve your margins, to increase your customer satisfaction, and to reduce your return rate.

## Agents

-   **Assortment Planner:** A specialist agent that can help you to plan your product assortment.
-   **Pricing/Promo Analyst:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your pricing and promotion analysis.
-   **Catalog Normalizer:** A specialist agent that can help you to normalize your product catalog.
-   **Returns Triage:** A specialist agent that can help you to triage your returns.
-   **CS Co-pilot:** A specialist agent that can provide real-time assistance to your customer support agents.
