The MindLab platform provides a suite of tools for the life sciences and pharma industries. Our platform is designed to help you to accelerate your research, to improve your clinical operations, and to ensure your compliance with regulatory requirements.

## Agents

-   **Protocol Designer:** A specialist agent that can help you to design your clinical trial protocols.
-   **Trial Ops Orchestrator:** A specialist agent that can help you to orchestrate your clinical trial operations.
-   **Safety Signal Detector:** A specialist agent that can help you to detect safety signals in your clinical trial data.
-   **Submission Packager (eCTD):** A specialist agent that can help you to package your electronic Common Technical Document (eCTD) submissions.

## Workflows

-   **Protocol drafting; site communications; pharmacovigilance narratives; dossier assembly.**

## Controls

-   **GxP:** The platform is designed to help you to comply with Good x Practice (GxP) regulations.
-   **Audit trails:** The platform provides a complete and immutable audit trail for every action that is taken.
-   **Role segregation:** The platform is designed to support role segregation.
