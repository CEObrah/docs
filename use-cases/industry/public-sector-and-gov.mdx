The MindLab platform provides a suite of tools for the public sector and government. Our platform is designed to help you to improve your case throughput, to reduce your backlog, and to increase your citizen satisfaction.

## Agents

-   **Case Manager Co-pilot:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your case management.
-   **Grant Writer:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your grant writing.
-   **Policy Harmonizer:** A specialist agent that can help you to harmonize your policies.
-   **FOIA Packager:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests.
-   **Citizen Services Bot:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your citizen services.

## Workflows

-   **Benefits case summaries; grant applications; policy diffing; FOIA bundles; service triage.**

## Controls

-   **Records laws:** The platform is designed to help you to comply with records laws.
-   **Accessibility:** The platform is designed to be accessible to all users.
-   **Security clearance scopes:** The platform is designed to support security clearance scopes.
