## Agents

-   **Case Manager Co-pilot:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your case management.
-   **Grant Writer:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your grant writing.
-   **Policy Harmonizer:** A specialist agent that can help you to harmonize your policies.
-   **FOIA Packager:** A specialist agent that can help you to with your Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests.
