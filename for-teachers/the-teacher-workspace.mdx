The Teacher Workspace is your central hub for managing your courses, content, and students. It is an AI-assisted environment designed to save you hours of administrative work, allowing you to focus on what you do best: teaching.

## Core Workspace Features

The Teacher Workspace is a modular system that provides a comprehensive suite of tools to support your daily workflows.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Content Generation">
    Upload your existing materials—PDFs, documents, web links—and your personal AI agent will help you generate quizzes, homework assignments, and lesson plans that are perfectly aligned with your curriculum.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Auto-Grading">
    For basic assignments, your AI agent can handle the grading for you, providing instant feedback to students and saving you hours of manual work. For more complex assignments, the agent can provide a first pass, highlighting key areas for your review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Student Summaries">
    Get a one-click executive summary of any student’s trajectory, including performance trends, behavioral notes, and intervention history. This is a powerful tool for preparing for parent-teacher meetings or internal reviews.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Communication Hub">
    Your workspace is your central hub for communicating with students and parents. Your AI agent can help you draft personalized messages, send automated reminders, and provide parents with clear, concise updates on their child's progress.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
