The MindLab EDU platform is a powerful tool for differentiated instruction. It provides you with the real-time, actionable insights you need to tailor your lessons to the specific needs of your students.

## From Data to Action

Your personal AI agent is constantly analyzing the performance data from your class. It will surface clusters of weakness and recommend specific, targeted interventions.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pacing Adjustments">
    Get recommendations on when to speed up and when to slow down, based on the real-time comprehension of your students.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Student Groupings">
    Your agent will suggest intelligent student groupings for collaborative work, based on their individual strengths and weaknesses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Supplemental Resources">
    Get recommendations for supplemental resources and activities that are perfectly tailored to the needs of individual students or small groups.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
