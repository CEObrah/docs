CADANCE™ is a declarative, versioned specification that turns intent into reproducible behavior. You ship behavior as YAML or JSON so runs are repeatable, auditable, and policy safe.

## The 7 Components of CADANCE™

CADANCE™ stands for Config, Agents, Data, Analysis, Norms, Controls, and Execution.

```yaml
Config:
  objective: "Analyze a financial news article for key points and sentiment."
  success_criteria: "A concise summary and a sentiment score (positive, negative, neutral)."
  budget:
    cost: 1.00
    latency: 60s
