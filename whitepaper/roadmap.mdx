## Overview

We are committed to the continuous improvement of the MindLab platform. Our roadmap is guided by a deep partnership with our customers, and we are constantly working to add new capabilities that will help you to succeed.

## Near Term (0-3 Months)

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Eval Corpus v1" icon="file-check-2">
    We are working to release the first version of our evaluation corpus, which will allow you to benchmark the performance of our platform against a wide range of tasks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CADANCE™ Editor" icon="file-pen">
    We are working to create a visual editor for the CADANCE™ Spec, which will make it even easier to define and manage your AI-driven workflows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Mid Term (3-9 Months)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Policy Learning Signals">
    We are working to add the ability for our platform to learn from the policy decisions that you make, which will allow it to become even more aligned with your organizational goals over time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Org-Wide Memory Services">
    We are working to add the ability for our platform to share memory across your entire organization, which will allow you to build a true, compounding knowledge asset.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Long Term (9-18 Months)

<Info>
We are working on a number of exciting new capabilities that will be coming to the platform in the long term, including formal verification of outputs and cross-workspace governance.
</Info>
