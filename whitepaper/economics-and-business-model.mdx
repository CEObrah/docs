## Overview

Our business model is designed to be simple, scalable, and aligned with the value we deliver to our customers.

## TCO Model

We believe that the true cost of an AI solution is not just the sticker price, but the total cost of ownership (TCO). Our TCO model is designed to be transparent and predictable, and it includes all of the costs associated with deploying and operating our platform.

## Pricing

Our pricing is designed to be simple and transparent. We offer a per-workspace subscription that includes a generous usage envelope, as well as a set of enterprise controls for managing your costs.
