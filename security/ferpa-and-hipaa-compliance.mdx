The MindLab platform is designed to meet the stringent data privacy and security requirements of the education and healthcare industries. We are fully compliant with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

## FERPA Compliance

FERPA is a federal law that protects the privacy of student education records. The MindLab platform is designed to help educational institutions to comply with FERPA by providing:

-   **Granular Permissions:** Control who has access to student data.
-   **Audit Trails:** Track all access to student data.
-   **Data Encryption:** Protect student data at rest and in transit.

## HIPAA Compliance

HIPAA is a federal law that protects the privacy and security of protected health information (PHI). The MindLab platform is designed to help healthcare organizations to comply with HIPAA by providing:

-   **Secure Infrastructure:** Our platform is built on a secure infrastructure that is designed to protect PHI.
-   **Access Controls:** Control who has access to PHI.
-   **Audit Trails:** Track all access to PHI.
