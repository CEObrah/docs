We understand that our customers in the education and healthcare sectors are subject to a unique set of regulatory requirements. The MindLab platform has been architected from the ground up to meet the stringent security and privacy standards of both FERPA and HIPAA.

## FERPA

The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) is a federal law that protects the privacy of student education records. The MindLab EDU platform is fully FERPA compliant.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Data Ownership">
    Your institution retains full ownership of all student data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Access Control">
    Our granular Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system ensures that only authorized users have access to student records.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Auditability">
    Every action taken on the platform is recorded in a tamper-evident log, providing a complete and immutable audit trail.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## HIPAA

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) is a federal law that protects the privacy and security of protected health information (PHI). The MindLab platform is HIPAA compliant.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="End-to-End Encryption">
    All PHI is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Strict Access Controls">
    Our RBAC system ensures that only authorized users have access to PHI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)">
    We will sign a BAA with all of our healthcare customers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
