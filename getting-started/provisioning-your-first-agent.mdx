Once you have created your workspace, the next step is to provision your first AI agent.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Agents Tab

From your workspace dashboard, navigate to the "Agents" tab in the main menu.

## Step 2: Add a New Agent

Click the "Add Agent" button. You will be presented with a list of available agents from the Expert Registry.

## Step 3: Configure Your Agent

Once you have selected an agent, you can configure it to meet the specific needs of your team.

-   **Name:** Give your agent a unique name.
-   **Permissions:** Define which members of your team have access to the agent.
-   **Data Sources:** Connect your agent to the relevant data sources.
