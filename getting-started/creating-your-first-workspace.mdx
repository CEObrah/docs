A workspace is a dedicated environment for a team or a project. It is a container for all of your data, agents, and workflows.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Workspaces Tab

From the MindLab dashboard, navigate to the "Workspaces" tab in the main menu.

## Step 2: Create a New Workspace

Click the "New Workspace" button. You will be prompted to provide a name and a description for your workspace.

## Step 3: Configure Your Workspace

Once you have created your workspace, you can configure it to meet the specific needs of your team.

-   **Data Sources:** Connect your workspace to a variety of data sources.
-   **Agents:** Provision a team of specialist AI agents.
-   **Permissions:** Define granular permissions for each member of your team.
