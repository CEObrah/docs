The Parent View is your secure, private portal into your child's educational journey. It is designed to give you the clarity and confidence you need to support your child's learning, without overwhelming you with information.

## Your Personal AI Agent

When you log in to the Parent View, you will be greeted by your personal AI agent. This is your dedicated copilot for navigating your child's academic world. You can ask it questions in plain English, and it will provide you with clear, concise answers that are grounded in your child's actual performance data.
